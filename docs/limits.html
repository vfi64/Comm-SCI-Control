Limits

Better control, not automatic truth.

Comm-SCI-Control can improve transparency, structure, and comparability. It cannot guarantee factual correctness.

Why Use Cases Glossary

No truth guarantee

The framework exposes uncertainty and verification needs; it does not replace fact-checking.

Formal overhead

More control often means longer output and lower spontaneity.

Display != evidence

Color and markers improve scanability; they are not proof by themselves.

Task fit matters

For simple everyday chat, Comm Stop or lighter settings may be the better option.
